Who We Are

A transparent skill-based esports platform built for competitive mobile gamers across India.

Our Story

Battlex11 is a premier skill-based esports platform specialized in organizing high-performance tournaments for Free Fire. We were founded with a simple goal: to provide a legitimate arena for tactical gamers to turn their skill into recognition.

In our tournaments, every outcome is a direct result of strategy, mechanical ability, and survival tactics. Players compete in a fair ecosystem where winners are determined by objective, in-game performance data—making technical skill the only path to the top.

We are dedicated to building a transparent and merit-based esports home for the Indian gaming community.

Our Mission

To create a premier destination for mobile esports in India — where skill is the only currency, fairness is non-negotiable, and every player gets an equal opportunity to compete and succeed.

We organize tournaments that are structured, professionally managed, and completely transparent from entry to result announcement. Every step of the process is designed to eliminate unfair advantage and ensure that the best player always wins.

Skill-Based. Always.

This is the most important thing to understand about Battlex11: we do not offer any form of gambling, betting, or games of chance.

Every tournament outcome on our platform is determined solely by player performance. The winner of a tournament is the player who performs best — measured by objective, verifiable in-game statistics. There is no element of luck, random draws, or chance-based mechanics involved in any of our competitions.

  • Winners determined by kills, survival, and in-game ranking
  • All results are verifiable through in-game screenshots and data
  • No random prize draws or luck-based selection of any kind
  • Platform complies with applicable Indian laws for skill gaming
