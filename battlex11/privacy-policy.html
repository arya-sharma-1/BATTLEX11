Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. Learn how Battlex11 protects your data and privacy.

At Battlex11, we are deeply committed to safeguarding your privacy. This policy details our procedures for collecting, utilizing, and securing your personal information responsibly, particularly during account management and payment processing.

1. The Information We Collect

Personal Data: When you register, enter tournaments, or engage in transactions, we collect specific details required to provide our services. This includes your name, email address, mobile number, and payment-related information.

Technical Data: We may also collect non-personal information automatically, such as your IP address, device type, and browser characteristics, to optimize your experience on our platform.

2. Use of Your Information

We utilize the data we collect to:

  • Facilitate tournament entry and manage competitive rankings.
  • Personalize your interaction with our platform and enhance site performance.
  • Securely process entry fees and manage reward distributions.
  • Communicate vital updates regarding your account, match schedules, and platform changes.
  • Fulfill our legal and regulatory compliance obligations.

3. Data Security & Protection

Battlex11 employs rigorous technical and administrative security protocols to shield your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. All sensitive payment processing is conducted through **PCI DSS-compliant** third-party gateways to ensure your financial credentials are never compromised on our servers.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We maintain a strict policy against selling or renting your personal data. We only share necessary information with verified payment processors and regulatory bodies when required to process your transactions or comply with legal mandates. All our partners are bound by strict confidentiality standards.

5. Integration of Third-Party Services

Our website features links to external services, such as payment gateways. Please be aware that Battlex11 does not control the privacy practices of these third-party entities. We strongly recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any external service you interact with through our platform.

6. Rights Over Your Information

You have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of the personal data we hold about you. Furthermore, you can opt-out of marketing communications at any time by following the instructions in our emails or by contacting our support team directly.

7. Legal Compliance

We reserve the right to disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a subpoena or government agency) to protect our legal rights or ensure the security of our users.

8. Policy Amendments

Battlex11 reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy as our services evolve. Any significant updates will be reflected by a change in the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to check back periodically to stay informed.

9. Acceptance of Policy

By using the Battlex11 website and participating in our tournaments, you signify your acceptance of this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our platform or making transactions.
